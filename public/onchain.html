What you're watching

What you see confirm here is the public API catching up, about a beat behind. It bundles an indexing buffer, the gasless relayer hop (it co-signs and pays the gas so you play with no wallet), and a polling interval, so it reads in roughly two seconds. That number is not finality.

True finality on MultiversX is sub-second, about 250ms intra-shard. We show that as an estimate, sourced from Supernova / Battle of Nodes telemetry, rather than measuring it through the API. The cross-chain comparison below uses each chain's real finality the same way. The transactions themselves are 100% real.
