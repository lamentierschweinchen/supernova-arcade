Bring your own game

Take any simple game and put it onchain: every move a real transaction, one tap, no wallet. Fork the open template (the Sprint stack) or roll your own. Meet the bar below and your game gets a permanent spot right here.

01One-tap gasless play. An ephemeral key in the browser, transactions relayed for free. No wallet, no install.
02An onchain score or shared state. A leaderboard or a shared tally the hub can read.
03Onchain scoring. A score is just the player's real transaction count, recorded on the chain for anyone to verify.
04The standard actions event. Emit arcadeAction so your game feeds this counter.
