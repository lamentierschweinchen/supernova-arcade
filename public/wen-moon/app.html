How it works

Start a run to get 1,000 NOVA. Pick a risk band (how likely a MOON is) and a wager (percentage of your bankroll). Hit Call it to fire a real gasless tx onchain. The contract draws the result from get_block_random_seed() (the MultiversX VRF-backed block seed) and applies it immediately: MOON grows your bankroll by the payout, RUG takes the wager. Press your luck and call again, or hit Cash Out to bank your score. Bust (bankroll hits 0) and the run is over.

Highscore = biggest single cash-out. Hub points accumulate across all your runs and count toward the Supernova leaderboard.

Why it's provably fair

The outcome is determined by get_block_random_seed(), a VRF-backed seed that is fixed by the block before your transaction is included. The derivation uses splitmix64 (the same hash used across every Supernova Arcade game):

seed = mix64( vrf_first8 XOR caller_last8 XOR mix64(run_id XOR mix64(call_id)) )
MOON if (seed % 10000) < moon_permil

Every call emits the seed that drove it in the transaction receipt. Click verify on explorer to inspect the block and recompute the result yourself. The contract source is open. The formula is always visible, not hidden in a black box.
