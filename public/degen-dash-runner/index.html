How it works
The world runs toward you and the feed flies past. Mint items are wins. Tap one to fire a real gasless transaction that grabs it before it slips behind you, +score. Red items are rugs, hacks and scams; leave them. Grab a red one and the contract docks you. The round seed and your final score are computed onchain, so the score can't be faked and every run is unique. It speeds up as it goes.
