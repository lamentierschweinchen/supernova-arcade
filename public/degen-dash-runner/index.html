How it works
You run the track and the feed rushes at you in three lanes. Swipe (or use the arrow keys) to switch lanes. Line a mint win up with your lane and you grab it automatically, firing a real gasless transaction, +score. Red items are rugs, hacks and scams; steer out of their lane before they reach you. Eat one and the contract docks you. The round seed and your final score are computed onchain, so the score can't be faked and every run is unique. It speeds up as it goes.
