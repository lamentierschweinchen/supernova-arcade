How it works
You run an endless track and the feed rushes at you in three lanes. Swipe (or arrow keys) to switch lanes, swipe up to jump, down to duck. A mint coin in your lane is grabbed automatically, firing a real gasless transaction, +score. Red hazards are rugs, hacks and gas spikes: switch out of a wall's lane, jump the low ones, duck the high ones. Hit one and the run ends. Your score is the coins you grabbed, computed onchain, so it can't be faked, and every run is seeded fresh. It speeds up the longer you last.
