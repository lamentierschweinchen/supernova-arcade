How it works

Mint cards are wins. Tap one to fire a real gasless transaction that collects it before it drops past the miss line, +score. Red cards are rugs and scams; leave them. Tap a red one and the contract docks you a penalty. The round seed and your final score are computed onchain, so the score can't be faked, and every run is unique. It speeds up as it goes.
